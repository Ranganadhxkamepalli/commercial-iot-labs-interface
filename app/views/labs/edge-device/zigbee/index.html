ZigBee Communication Lab

In this module, you will learn how to use ZigBee radios to transfer data between your Intel® Edison and your Intel® IoT Gateway.

We will be using XBee's Series 1 (also called XBee 802.15.4) based radios and xbee-api node module to establish communication with XBee devices.
